Agglomerator¶

Plant::ProcessEquipment::Agglomerator

Overview¶

Concrete Class

RDL: AGGLOMERATOR (http://sandbox.dexpi.org/rdl/Agglomerator)

A machine that is capable of agglomerating. It is usually vertically aligned.
