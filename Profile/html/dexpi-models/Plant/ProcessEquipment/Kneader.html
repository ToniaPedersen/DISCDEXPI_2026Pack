Kneader¶

Plant::ProcessEquipment::Kneader

Overview¶

Concrete Class

RDL: KNEADER (http://sandbox.dexpi.org/rdl/Kneader)

A machine that is capable of mixing and working into a uniform mass by, or as if by, folding, pressing, and stretching.
