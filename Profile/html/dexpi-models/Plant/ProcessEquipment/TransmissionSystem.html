TransmissionSystem¶

Plant::ProcessEquipment::TransmissionSystem

Overview¶

Concrete Class

RDL: TRANSMISSION SYSTEM (http://data.posccaesar.org/rdl/RDS13105034)

A rotating equipment system that can transmit mechanical power from its Driver to driven Plant.ProcessEquipment.Equipment by means of an assembly of transmission components and auxiliaries.
