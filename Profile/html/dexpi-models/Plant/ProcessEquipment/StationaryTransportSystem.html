Plant::ProcessEquipment::StationaryTransportSystem

Overview¶

Concrete Class

RDL: STATIONARY TRANSPORT SYSTEM (http://sandbox.dexpi.org/rdl/StationaryTransportSystem)

A transport system that is intended to transport, store or load/unload material and that, as a whole, remains in one place.
